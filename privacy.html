Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CreatorOps is an Instagram automation application that helps creators automate responses to Instagram comments and Story replies.

Information We Process

When an Instagram Professional Account is connected, CreatorOps may process:

  • Instagram account ID and username
  • Instagram media and Story identifiers
  • Comments and direct messages used to trigger automations
  • Instagram scoped user identifiers
  • Automation settings created by the account owner
  • Webhook events and delivery status

CreatorOps never collects or stores Instagram passwords. Authentication is performed using Meta's official APIs.

How We Use Information

  • Process Instagram automations
  • Detect comments and Story replies
  • Send automated responses configured by the account owner
  • Prevent duplicate replies
  • Improve application reliability

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Information is only processed as required to operate the application and communicate with Meta's official APIs.

Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to operate the application and troubleshoot issues. Users may request deletion at any time.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored information. Secrets such as access tokens are never intentionally exposed publicly.

Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your stored information at any time. Please refer to the Data Deletion page for instructions.
